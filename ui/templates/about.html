  • About Nikhil

    Driving Business Growth Through Data-Driven Strategies.

Beginnings

I was born in Bengaluru, the garden city and the silicon valley of India, to an Aerospace Engineer and Architect. As you can imagine, the pressure to make something of my life was enormous. So while I was in my engineering, I got selected for a competitive program called Entrepreneurs on Training where my project, Healing Through Food, got funded where I designed disease specific food products for hospital patients.

Driven by competition and curiosity to learn everything under the sun, I founded a gaming cafe at the age of 18. As people began to realize my software skills, I started getting consulting projects for web development which led me on the path of technology.

Educational Foundation

I hold a MBA in Business Analytics from McMaster University's DeGroote School of Business and an Bachelor's of Engineering degree, Computer Science from NIE Institute of Technology, providing me with a solid academic foundation.

During my studies, I undertook projects such as developing a custom ResearchGPT for business research and collaborating on an IT project management initiative with Scotia Bank, showcasing my ability to apply advanced analytics and AI in real-world business contexts.

Professional Experience

My diverse professional background encompasses significant roles in data analytics, IT consulting, and digital marketing.

Notable experiences include contributing to analytics projects at Britnell Venture, aiding workplace safety at the Workplace Safety & Insurance Board (WSIB), leading product development efforts at KnowQuest, and spearheading digital growth strategies as a Digital Marketing Head at moneyHOP. These roles have honed my ability to manage and execute complex projects effectively while leveraging technology for business growth.
